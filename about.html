About Caru Cars

Miami's trusted Buy Here Pay Here dealership since day one.

We Believe Everyone Deserves a Reliable Car

Caru Cars was built on a simple idea: getting a car shouldn't be complicated, stressful, or out of reach. We know that life happens — credit scores don't tell the whole story, and one setback shouldn't keep you from getting to work, school, or taking care of your family.

Located on NW 7th Avenue in the heart of Miami, we serve a diverse community of hardworking people. Our team speaks English and Spanish, and we treat every customer like family.

With our Buy Here Pay Here program, we finance directly — no banks, no credit checks, no games. Just honest deals and flexible payments that work for real life.
